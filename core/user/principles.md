# Principles
*What you believe and how you operate — the commitments that guide your decisions.*

---

*These aren't goals or habits. They're the values that stay stable when everything else changes. Write them in the first person, in your own voice. The AI will use these to understand what "a good decision" means for you.*

---

## How to write a principle

A useful principle is:
- Specific enough to actually constrain a decision
- Yours — not something you copied from a motivational poster
- Worth the AI knowing when it advises you

**Example:** "I don't optimize for comfort. If I'm avoiding something, that's usually a signal I should move toward it."
