# LifeSphere

> A context-aware identity layer for AI — scoped to your whole life.

LifeSphere is the widest-angle lens. It's where you bring questions that cross domains — career and life, relationships and purpose, short-term and long-term. The AI here knows the full picture of who you are.

---

## What lives here

```
core/           ← identity and machinery
  user/         ← who you are across all contexts
  ai/           ← your AI persona for this Sphere
  skills/       ← onboard, reflect, fork, replicate
layers/
  philosophy/   ← your examined worldview and operating principles
  wellness/     ← energy, health, routines
surface/        ← artifacts from your life work
records/        ← decisions, reflections, history
```

## When to use LifeSphere

- You're thinking about a major life decision
- You want to reflect across domains — work and life, now and future
- You're working through something that doesn't fit in a more focused Sphere
- You want to examine your principles or philosophy
- You're doing annual or seasonal reviews

For deep in-the-weeds work on a specific domain, consider a focused Sphere (WorkSphere, etc.) and switch back here for the high-level view.

## Growing your LifeSphere

The philosophy and wellness layers are pre-installed. Other layers to consider adding:
- `layers/financial/` — if you want financial context in your life thinking
- `layers/projects/` — personal projects and creative work
- `layers/family/` — family context, caregiving, relationships

Install layers by creating a directory under `layers/` with a `README.md` describing what it holds and a `context.md` for the actual content.
