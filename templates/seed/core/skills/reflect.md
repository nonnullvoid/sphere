# Skill: Reflect
*Sphere evolution — review, refine, let it grow.*

---

## When to run this

Run this:
- Every few months, or when your life has shifted significantly
- When the AI's responses feel slightly off, like it's working from a stale model of you
- When you want to consciously update your principles or persona
- After a major transition — new job, new city, new chapter

---

## What this skill does

Reflect is a structured review of your Sphere. It surfaces what's still true, what's outdated, and what's missing. The AI leads — you respond, adjust, and update.

---

## Run it

Tell your AI: **"Run the reflect skill"** or **"Let's do a Sphere review."**

---

## Instructions for AI

When this skill is invoked:

1. **Read** all files in `core/user/`, `core/ai/persona.md`, and any active `layers/`
2. **Surface observations** — what feels current, what feels stale, what's conspicuously absent. Be specific: "Your profile says X but you've been talking about Y a lot lately."
3. **Work through files conversationally** — propose edits, confirm, write. Don't rewrite wholesale; surgical updates are better.
4. **Update persona.md last** — after reviewing the user files, reflect on how the relationship and working style has evolved. Update the AI's self-description accordingly.
5. **Log a decision** — if any significant change was made to core identity, add an entry to `records/decisions.md`

### What to look for

- Profile facts that have changed (job, location, life stage)
- Principles that are no longer held or need refinement
- Preferences that have shifted based on observed working patterns
- Roles that are dormant or new roles that have emerged
- Layers that should be added or removed

### Tone

Honest and direct. This is a checkpoint, not a celebration. Surface the real gaps. The user may push back — that's fine, it's their Sphere.
